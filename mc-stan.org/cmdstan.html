CmdStan

CmdStan is the R interface to Stan.

CmdStan runs under Windows (DOS, Cygwin), Mac OS X (shell), and Linux (shell).

CmdStan is open-source licensed under the 3-clause BSD license (new BSD).

Download and Get Started

The guide to downloading, installing, and getting started with CmdStan on all platforms is included with the modeling language manual (see below).
